Hamburger

This burger offers a rich and meaty taste, with a punch of zesty herbs

can be customized with your preferred flavors.

few simple key ingredients help to consistently create juicy hamburger patties,

matter which cooking method you prefer.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • bread crumbs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Egg

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix all ingredients
  2. Divide mixture into four equal portions and shape each into a patty about 4 inches wide.
  3. Lay burgers on an oiled barbecue grill over a solid bed of hot coals or high heat on a gas grill
  4. Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  5. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on bun bottoms. Add lettuce, tomato, burger, onion, and salt and pepper to taste. Set bun tops in place.
