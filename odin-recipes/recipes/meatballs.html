Meatballs

This homemade meatball recipe is a Betty classic, and for great reason!

For generations, home cooks have relied on this hearty meatball recipe to show some skills the kitchen.

All it takes to achieve this meaty main dish is eight basic ingredients

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • bread crumbs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the panade
  2. Combine remaining ingredients
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Prep oven and baking sheet
  5. Form the meatballs
  6. Bake
